Results-Driven Digital Marketing for Irish Businesses

Most small businesses in Ireland know they need digital marketing. The challenge is knowing where to start, which channels will actually work, and how to avoid wasting money on tactics that sound good but deliver nothing. That is exactly where I come in.

I have spent over a decade running digital campaigns for brands including Samsung, Bayer, and a range of Irish SMEs. I bring that same strategic thinking to every project, whether you are a startup launching your first Google Ads campaign or an established business looking to overhaul your content strategy.

My approach is straightforward: understand your business goals, identify where your audience spends time online, build campaigns around those insights, and measure everything so you know exactly what is working.

Google Ads, Meta Ads & Amazon Advertising

Paid advertising is the fastest way to put your business in front of the right people. I manage campaigns across Google Search, Google Display, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and Amazon, depending on where your customers are most likely to convert.

Every campaign starts with proper keyword research and audience targeting. I structure ad groups to maximise quality scores, write ad copy that speaks to buyer intent, and set up conversion tracking so we can attribute revenue to specific campaigns. Ongoing optimisation covers bid adjustments, negative keywords, A/B testing of creatives, and landing page improvements.

For e-commerce businesses selling on Amazon, I also manage Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display campaigns, helping you win the buy box and increase product visibility in a competitive marketplace.

SEO & Content Strategy

Search engine optimisation is the foundation of long-term online visibility. I take a practical approach to SEO that focuses on what actually moves the needle: technical health, strategic keyword targeting, and high-quality content that earns links and builds authority.

Technical SEO

I audit your site for crawl errors, broken links, page speed issues, mobile usability problems, and indexing gaps. Fixing these fundamentals ensures search engines can discover and rank your content properly.

Keyword Research & On-Page Optimisation

I research the terms your potential customers are searching for, map those keywords to relevant pages, and optimise title tags, meta descriptions, headings, and internal links. The goal is to match your content with genuine search demand in your market.

Content Planning & Production

I develop content calendars that align blog posts, landing pages, and resource guides with your target keywords and business objectives. Whether you need help writing content or simply need a plan your team can execute, I can build a strategy that compounds over time.

Email Marketing

Services include list segmentation, welcome series design, abandoned cart recovery flows, newsletter templates, and performance reporting. I work with platforms including Mailchimp, Klaviyo, and Brevo to build systems that run efficiently without constant manual effort.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does digital marketing cost in Ireland?

Digital marketing costs vary widely depending on scope. A small business might invest between EUR 500 and EUR 2,000 per month for a managed campaign covering SEO and paid ads. Freelance consultants typically offer more flexible pricing than agencies, and many offer project-based or retainer models that suit smaller budgets.

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Paid advertising can generate leads within days of launching. SEO is a longer-term investment, with meaningful ranking improvements typically appearing after 3 to 6 months. Content marketing and email sequences build momentum over time and deliver compounding returns.

Which digital marketing platform is best for my business?

The best platform depends on your audience and goals. Google Ads captures high-intent search traffic. Meta Ads excel at brand awareness and retargeting. LinkedIn is strong for B2B. Amazon Ads suit e-commerce brands. A good consultant will audit your situation and recommend the right channel mix.

Do I need a big budget to start digital marketing?

No. Many small businesses in Ireland start with modest budgets of a few hundred euros per month and scale up as they see returns. The key is to focus spending on the channels most likely to reach your target audience, measure everything, and optimise continuously.
