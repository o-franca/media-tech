MVP Development for Startups

An MVP is the fastest path from idea to market validation. Instead of building everything you imagine your product could be, you build the smallest version that solves the core problem and put it in front of real users. Their behaviour tells you what to build next.

My MVP Process

  1. Discovery: We define the core problem your product solves and identify the target user. I help you strip away nice-to-have features and focus on what matters most.
  2. Design: I create wireframes and a basic design system that communicates your brand while keeping development fast. No over-engineered Figma files; just enough design to build something users want to interact with.
  3. Build: Using AI-assisted and no-code tools, I build the product in short sprints, sharing progress regularly so you can provide feedback as we go.
  4. Launch: I handle deployment, domain setup, analytics integration, and app store submission where applicable. You get a live product, not a prototype sitting on a staging server.
  5. Iterate: Based on real user data and feedback, we prioritise improvements and build the next version.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you build an app without coding?

Yes. Modern no-code and low-code platforms make it possible to build fully functional apps without writing traditional code. Tools like Bolt, Cursor, and various AI-assisted builders allow you to create web apps, mobile apps, and interactive products that can be deployed to real users. The result is a working product at a fraction of the time and cost of traditional development.

How much does app development cost in Ireland?

Traditional app development in Ireland can cost anywhere from EUR 15,000 to EUR 100,000 or more. AI-assisted and no-code development dramatically reduces this. A functional MVP can often be built for EUR 2,000 to EUR 8,000 depending on complexity, with the ability to iterate and scale based on real user feedback rather than upfront assumptions.

What is an MVP?

An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) is the simplest version of your product that solves the core problem for your target users. It includes only the essential features needed to test your idea with real people. The purpose is to validate demand before investing heavily in full development.

How long does it take to build an app?

With AI-assisted and no-code approaches, a basic MVP can be built in 2 to 4 weeks. A more feature-rich application might take 6 to 12 weeks. Traditional development timelines are significantly longer. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of the product, the number of integrations, and how quickly feedback loops are incorporated.
