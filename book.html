About the Book

Let's Get Together promotes meaningful and fun conversations between friends, partners, and people who are getting to know each other. It's an essential tool for those looking to deepen their relationships and foster connections.

The questions go beyond everyday small talk, exploring thought-provoking topics designed to elicit thoughts, feelings, and personal experiences. From personal preferences to deeper inquiries about values, dreams, and life experiences — some questions may be surprising and even unconventional.

Perfect for a quiet night at home, a romantic date, a gathering of friends, or as an icebreaker in any social setting.

About the Author

J.P. Franca is a Brazilian-Irish communications professional and product builder based in Cork, Ireland. Inspired by the cultural differences discovered through his relationship with his Irish wife, he wrote Let's Get Together as a way to help people bridge gaps and find common ground through meaningful conversations.
