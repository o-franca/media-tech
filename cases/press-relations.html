BH Airport — Strategic Communications

Strategic communications for Brazil's Belo Horizonte International Airport

Project Overview

As a corporate communications consultant for BH Airport, I managed a wide range of communications activities for Belo Horizonte International Airport, one of Brazil's major airport concessions. My work included press advisory (assessoria de imprensa), internal communications campaigns, video production for the airport, and corporate reporting.

Over my time with BH Airport, I produced 100+ editions of corporate publications — including the weekly newsletter "Nosso Mundo Semanal", the "Boletim Seguranca Integrada" security bulletin, the "Revista BH Airport" magazine, and the "Revista Seguranca Integrada". I also produced 50+ corporate videos, managed press advisory and media relations, coordinated events, co-authored an industry paper on airport branding, and led the production of the annual corporate report.

Key Results

  • Produced the 2019 Annual Report covering 11.17 million passengers and a 102% EBITDA increase
  • Co-authored an industry paper on airport brand attributes post-privatisation
  • BH Airport won the regional Aberje Award for its 'Year of Experiences' project
  • Revenue diversified across 7 business lines, growing 5% year-over-year to R$188.7M
  • 100+ editions of corporate publications (newsletter, bulletins, magazines)
  • 50+ corporate videos produced for internal and external communications
  • Managed press advisory and media relations across TV, print, and digital
  • Coordinated corporate events and press conferences
  • Created 'Momento Conexao' — weekly employee alignment sessions
  • Developed internal communications campaigns for airport employees
  • Supported the 'PARE DENTRO' parking campaign and Uber partnership launch
